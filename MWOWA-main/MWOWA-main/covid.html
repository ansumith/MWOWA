COVID GUIDELINES FOR MAHAVEER WILLET- UPDATED IN SEPTEMBER 2020
1. NO PRE-APPROVAL will be required for VISITORS, GUESTS, MAIDS, HOUSE HELP, CAR CLEANERS and
SERVICE PROVIDERS. It will be MANDATORY TO FOLLOW COVID GUIDELINES- TEMPERATURE CHECK, HAND
SANITISATION, MASK AND SOCIAL DISTANCING.
3. Vegetable vendor, fruit vendor, food stalls will be allowed in the premises after conforming to MWOWA
guidelines on COVID PROTOCOL. The vendors must comply with the form in this regard available with the
security and preapproved by MWOWA. SOCIAL DISTANCING AND WEARING MASK IS MANDATORY. WE WILL
STRICTLY FOLLOW - “NO MASK, NO SHOPPING” POLICY.
4. Milk, essential items, online deliveries will be restricted at the main gate. Trolley is available with the
security to facilitate easy transport of materials. Gas and water delivery can happen at your doorstep- after
following MASK, SOCIAL DISTANCING measures.
5. All amenities in Willet premises remain open among the residents, but please follow appropriate social
distancing protocols. CLUBHOUSE AND OPEN AMENITIES AREA is now open to residents for booking.
6. Residents MUST REPORT any health issues or symptoms of cough, cold, breathlessness and high fever
immediately to MWOWA to help the society in taking proactive preventive measures.
MWOWA is committed to the safety and security of the residents and would request your active participation
for the same. Please be entrusted that MWOWA has taken adequate measures to maintain hygiene and
sanitation in the premises. Please get in touch with the association through mwillet.assn@gmail.com.
